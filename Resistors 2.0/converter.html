Resistor Convertion

The amount of voltage that can pass through the resistors highly depends on the value that can be decrypted on it's Bands. The least amount of band that is present on the resistors is four and the greatest amount is six. In order to know the values, if it is a four banded resistor the first two bands are the significant figures, The third one is the multiplier, finaly the last one is the tolerance. Also in addition if it's a five Banded resistor, the only change is that you will add another band on the positive side of the resistor and it is also functions as significant figure. Lastly the six Banded one, you will simply just another band on the negative side of the resistor and it signifies.
